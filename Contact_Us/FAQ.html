frequently asked questions

HOW DO I BOOK A STUDIO?

To book a studio or for any further information, please contact the Studios Team by filling out the form along with a message. You will then receive our rates and booking information by e-mail.

 HOW MUCH IS A RECORDING SESSION?

You can rent the studio by the hour, day, or week. Every session is different, so the amount of studio time you need and the final cost will ultimately depend on your project, budget, and own preparations. Let us know what you need and we will be able to give you an estimate. Contact us here.
HOW DO I CONTACT STUDIO?

We take all booking requests and initial contact via our Contact Us form found here. For non-booking inquiries:
info@studiok.com

 DO YOU HAVE ACCOMMODATION?

We don’t have our own accommodation at the studio, but our clients get special rates at nearby hotels and villas. Contact us for more info.
CAN YOU PRODUCE MY MUSIC?

If you need help producing your songs, our great team can do that. We don’t have a fixed rate per song, but we can offer an á la carte menu for your music, including pre-production and live-band recordings to house remixes. Please fill out our Contact Us form found here, and we will send you examples of our services.

 CAN YOU MIX AND MASTER MY SONG?

We deliver a wide variety of mixing and mastering services. For more detailed information and prices, please contact us here.
